Frequently Asked Questions

How long do deposits take?

Cryptocurrency deposits typically require 1-3 network confirmations and may take 10-30 minutes. Bank transfers may take 1-3 business days.

What are the withdrawal fees?

Withdrawal fees vary by cryptocurrency and network conditions. You can view the current fee before confirming any withdrawal.

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Go to Account Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and follow the setup instructions using Google Authenticator or Authy.
